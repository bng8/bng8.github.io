EK210 - Light Collimator Tester

Over the course of a semester, I worked in a group of five students to design a testing apparatus for Agira Optic's light collimator. We designed the testing apparatus to hold the light collimator on a rotating platform below a lux meter, created from a light sensor connected to an Arduino, attached to a rotating lever arm. The idea was test the light intensity generated from the light collimator in set intervals within a semicircular range about the light collimator. For my portion of the work, I handled the electronics and controls of the project: I managed to load a sketch onto an Arduino that allowed the microcontroller to rotate the platform and the lever arm and read in data from the light sensor that was simulataneously written to an SD card. Had we had more time to work on the project, we would have like to expand on the data analysis.

EC327 - Wisely

Over the course of one week, worked in a team of four to create a choose-your-own-adventure Android app with a college theme. For the project, I managed our GitHub repository, created all of the vector images used in the app, as well as coded a majority of the functionality: switching between activities, changing the color of the button when clicked, having the device vibrate when a choice is made, playing an audio file at the end of the game, and storing a generated score into a database.
