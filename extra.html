Boston University Club Fencing Team

In my freshman year at BU, I decided to check out the fencing club and it was one of the best decisions that I have ever made! I fell in love with the sport and the team! This year, I have the fortune to serve on the Executive Board as the club treasurer. Over the past three years, I have practiced with the club and have been a regular member of the competition team that represents the university at inter-collegiate tournaments.

Hackathons

I recently discovered the joy of participating in hackathons. Originally, I was put off by the idea of spending 36 hours straight working on a project, but in fact, after having attended HackHavard 2016 and YHack 2016, I have come too feel the opposite of my initial expectations! I love the idea of setting aside the time to simply explore and learn new topics and concepts and attempting to combine the new skills to create a project of my choosing. The absolute freedom associated with hackathons is overwhelmingly appealing to me now and I hope to attend many more in the forseeable future!

BostonHacks

This year, I am helping to organize the Boston University Hackathon, BostonHacks, which will be held in the upcoming Spring semester! We anticipate hosting more than 350 collegiate hackers from across North America, in addition to any local students who register! I am currently coordinating the transportation and logistics of the event!
